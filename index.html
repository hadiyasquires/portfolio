Frequently asked questions

What is your design process like?

I begin by understanding your goals and users’ needs. Then, I brainstorm ideas, sketch designs, and create prototypes to refine and improve. Your feedback helps shape a well-crafted, user-friendly final product.

What are your design principles?

My design principles focus on making things clear, simple, and centered around the user. I aim for intuitive navigation, clear communication, and attractive visuals that enhance user experience while meeting user’s needs.”

What industries have you worked with?

I’ve worked across various industries so far and look forward to expanding into more. My experience includes food and beverage, advocacy/political and retail with a keen interest in exploring new sectors.”
